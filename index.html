The Forge Calculator

The Forge Wiki

Our The Forge Calculator is an all-in-one tool, designed to help you out when Forging a Weapon or a piece of Armor in The Forge.

The Calculator is built both for beginners, using it to enhance their knowledge of the Forging system, and for already experienced players seeking optimal builds and trait setups.

Overview

Instead of manually calculating which Ore combinations will grant you the best Modifiers, or the number of Ores needed to obtain the highest percentages of their corresponding Traits, simply use the Calculator to process your inputs and present the results of a potential craft in real time.

When adding Ores to our Calculator tool, you’ll be able to see:

  • Which Weapon or Armor Class you’ll craft.
  • Optimal Ore amounts.
  • Final Modifier multipliers.
  • Whether Ore Traits will be active.
  • How strong the applied Traits will be.
  • Optimal number of Ores needed to increase the chances of crafting a specific Weapon or Armor set.
